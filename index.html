Identity Verification

Securely verify your identity document with guided camera capture

Good Lighting

Natural or bright indoor light. Avoid harsh shadows or glare on the ID.

Flat Document Placement

Place your document on a flat, dark surface. All corners must be visible.

Clear Selfie

Face camera directly. Remove glasses, hats, or masks. Neutral expression.

Proper Distance

Hold phone 20–30 cm from document. For selfie, arm's length is ideal.

Stay Steady

Keep both hands steady while capturing. The app guides you in real time.

Avoid

No blurry images, cut-off documents, dark rooms, or reflective surfaces.
