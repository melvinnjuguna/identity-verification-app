Identity Verification

Securely verify your identity document with guided camera capture

Good Lighting
Use natural or bright indoor light. Avoid harsh shadows or glare on the ID.
Flat ID Placement
Place your ID on a flat, dark surface. Hold it steady with all corners visible.
Clear Selfie
Face the camera directly. Remove glasses, hats, or masks. Neutral expression.
Proper Distance
Hold the phone 20-30 cm from the ID. For selfie, arm's length is ideal.
Stay Steady
Keep both hands steady while capturing. The app will guide you in real time.
Avoid
No blurry images, cut-off documents, dark rooms, or reflective surfaces.
